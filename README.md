# Executive Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Executive Sales Performance Dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard analyzes sales data from the Superstore dataset and provides actionable business insights through data visualization and storytelling techniques.

The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support decision-making and business strategy.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Measure profitability across categories and products
- Identify top-performing regions and customer segments
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on profit
- Generate business recommendations based on data insights
- Demonstrate effective data storytelling using Power BI

---

## Dataset

Dataset Used:

**Sample Superstore Dataset**

- Sales
- Profit

---

## Business Insights

### Key Findings

- Technology is the highest revenue-generating category.
- The West region contributes the largest share of sales.
- Consumer customers generate the majority of purchases.
- Copiers and Phones are among the most profitable products.
- Standard Class is the most frequently used shipping method.
- Excessive discounts negatively impact profitability.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing technology products.
- Reduce aggressive discounting strategies.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
- Improve profitability of lower-margin categories.
- Strengthen customer retention initiatives.
